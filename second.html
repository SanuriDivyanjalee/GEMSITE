Gem jewellery is a type of jewellery that features precious or semi-precious gemstones. Gemstones are minerals that have been cut and polished to be used in jewellery. Some of the most popular gemstones used in jewellery include diamonds, rubies, emeralds, sapphires, and pearls. Gemstone jewellery can come in a variety of forms, including necklaces, bracelets, earrings, and rings. The value of gemstone jewellery is often determined by the rarity and quality of the gemstones used.

Gemstone jewellery has been worn for centuries and has been associated with wealth, power, and status. In ancient times, gemstones were believed to have healing properties and were often worn as talismans or amulets. Today, gemstone jewellery is still highly valued for its beauty and symbolism. Diamonds are often associated with love and commitment, while rubies are associated with passion and courage.

Gemstone jewellery can be found in a variety of styles to suit different tastes and occasions. Some people prefer classic designs that feature a single gemstone set in gold or silver, while others prefer more modern designs that incorporate multiple gemstones or unique settings.

Overall, gemstone jewellery is a timeless and valuable addition to any collection. Whether you're looking for a special gift or a statement piece for yourself, there's sure to be a piece of gemstone jewellery that will suit your needs.

Nawarathna Gemstones

Nawaratna gemstones are a combination of nine different gemstones that are believed to bring good luck, prosperity, and happiness to the wearer. The nine gemstones include diamond, ruby, emerald, blue sapphire, yellow sapphire, hessonite, cat's eye, pearl, and coral. According to traditional Hindu astrology, each of these gemstones represents a planet and wearing them together in a specific arrangement can balance the energies of the planets and bring positive effects to the wearer's life. Nawaratna jewelry is popular in South Asian countries such as Srilanka and India and is often worn as a talisman or amulet for good fortune.

Birthstones

Birthstones are gemstones that are associated with a person's birth month. Each month has a specific gemstone assigned to it, and wearing one's birthstone is said to bring good luck and protection. The concept of birthstones dates back to ancient times, with the first recorded use of them being in the Bible. Over time, different cultures have assigned different gemstones to each month, but the modern list of birthstones was standardized by the National Association of Jewelers in 1912.

The list includes:
Garnet for January
Amethyst for February
Aquamarine for March
Diamond for April
Emerald for May
Pearl or Alexandrite for June
Ruby for July
Peridot for August
Sapphire for September
Opal or Tourmaline for October
Topaz or Citrine for November
Turquoise or Tanzanite for December.
